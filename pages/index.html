Welcome to EMC
We provide user-friendly access to state-of-the-art equipment, service and expertise in electron microscopy.

Our goal is to promote the development of researchers that are knowledgeable, confident and comfortable in the use of electron microscopes.

  • When you login the data you got transferred from the EM should be visible within your directory.
  • When creating directories in the emc1.slate, the directory should be named as <iu email>_<current date>
  • Date format mmddyyyy
  • E.g. eabeysin@iu.edu_11302021
  • An individual directory for each collection you have.
  • Within the directory you can view the files in your collection
  • Each file
    1. You can Download
    2. You can add them to your own collection group
    3. You can add notes to each file, collection for your reference
    4. You can view available metadata for each file, directory
3. Use the portal to access the images, meta data and share

Do you want to share your collection? Your files?

  1. If you have permission to share, you would have the button enabled.
  2. If you don't have permission to share, please contact your PI or Editor of the lab.
  3. When you share, you can share with Editor permissions or View permissions.
  4. Editor permission will enable you to share with others, view, download.
  5. View permissions will allow you to view and download. No sharing.
